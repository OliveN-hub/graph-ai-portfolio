About the course

Generative AI for Work & Research (8‑week live cohort)

A guided, hands‑on program covering LLM fundamentals, no‑code website/docs creation, AI‑assisted data analysis, text/literature workflows, custom GPTs & chatbots, task automation, communication & outreach, and a capstone showcase.

Bootcamp features

  • Weekly live workshops & help sessions
  • Private community + personalized feedback
  • Capstone coaching & public showcase
  • Shareable certificate
  • Lifetime access to tutorial library

Grading

Online quizzes15%
Workshop assignments35%
Attendance & participation10%
Final project40%

Passing grade: 80%. Attend at least 5 workshops for full attendance credit.

Module 1: Websites, Documents & Presentations

How LLMs work; generate Markdown/LaTeX/HTML; build simple research websites; use Markdown efficiently; AI tools for slides/posters.

Module 2: AI‑Automated Data Analysis

Use ChatGPT Code Interpreter and Julius; build automated dashboards; process/transform datasets; spreadsheet automations; visualizations & reports.

Module 3: GenAI for Research & Text Analysis

Efficient literature reviews (Elicit/LiteRev); text classification; sentiment analysis; knowledge maps; summarization pipelines; information synthesis.

Module 4: Custom GPTs & Chatbots

Chat architecture, context windows & RAG; custom GPTs; Botpress chatbots; integrate knowledge bases with your research materials.

Module 5: AI for Task Automation

Agent‑based systems; email/response automations; browser control agents; orchestration of multi‑step workflows.

Module 6: GenAI for Communication & Outreach

AI‑generated research summaries, figures/diagrams, video abstracts; prompt engineering for visualization; content scheduling.

Module 7: Capstone Project & Showcase

Progress checks, peer feedback, final presentation, and public Q&A.
